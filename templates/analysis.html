Key Findings
What the Data Tells Us
📈
Consistent Warming Signal
Every decade since the 1970s has been warmer than the previous one. The 2010s recorded the highest decade-average in the 165-year dataset, with no sign of reversal.
⚡
Rate of Change Doubling
Post-1980 warming is approximately twice the rate of the full 20th century average — a clear non-linear acceleration tied to compounding greenhouse gas accumulation.
❄️
Winter Amplification Effect
Winter months show disproportionate warming vs summer in most northern latitudes — contributing to earlier springs, later frosts, and disrupted agricultural cycles globally.
